Cleaning the dust from your glasses
Ever tried cleaning the dust from your heart?
Washing the eyes twice and thrice,
What about the lust you carry in your mind? -Dikku
You remember last night when you said no?
You left the lame and pained hands of mine.
You left me when I was so in need of yours.
The roots which helped you to grow in every single stage of your life,
and how it was easy for you to leave me in a time?
You remember when you were a little boy and I without your father tried to make everything available for you.
Never let you know any pain of mine ?
Now my pale face, shrinked skin and dark age has the need of the bridge to walk till my salvation.
Your mom needs you!! -Dikku
I wish I could fill so much love in people,
so when if in future i would ask for water
they would bring me the shake. - Dikku
